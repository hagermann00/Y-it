Quick setup

  1. Open every tool you will touch today in its own browser tab.
  2. Pin this companion page so it always stays visible.
  3. Drag the window beside your active work tab or use split-screen mode.
  4. As you switch tabs, copy prompts and notes from here to stay consistent.
